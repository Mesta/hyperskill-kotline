Cities comparison Open on Hyperskill

Imagine we want to compare the number of residents in two cities: New York and London. We implement the City class.

According to the real-life rules, there are some restrictions. If the number is out of range, it's corrected: the population of the city will be zero for less than zero and 50 million for greater than 50_000_000.

Finish the implementation of City class that will follow this logic.


Sample Input:
-5
200000
Sample Output:
London
